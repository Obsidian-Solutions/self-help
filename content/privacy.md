Last updated: January 26, 2026

At **MindFull**, we take your privacy and mental wellness data with extreme seriousness. This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use our theme and platform.

## 1. Data We Collect

As a privacy-focused theme, we prioritize local data storage.

- **Authentication Data:** Name and Email (Stored in your browser's LocalStorage).
- **Wellness Data:** Mood check-ins, journal entries, and course progress (Stored in your browser's LocalStorage).
- **Preferences:** Theme settings and notification choices.

## 2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the services offered by MindFull:

- Personalizing your dashboard.
- Tracking your progress through mental health courses.
- Providing a private space for your digital journal.

## 3. Storage and Security

**MindFull is designed to be a "Zero-Server" prototype by default.** This means:

- Your journal entries and mood logs never leave your device unless you connect a custom backend.
- We do not have access to your passwords or private reflections.

## 4. Your Rights

You have the full right to:

- Access your data (visible in your settings and dashboard).
- Delete your data (by clearing your browser cache or using the "Reset Account" feature in settings).
- Export your data.

## 5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@mindfull.com.

This Cookie Policy explains how **MindFull** uses cookies and similar technologies (like LocalStorage) to provide, customize, and improve our services.

## 1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser. While "cookies" is the common term, MindFull primarily uses **LocalStorage**, which allows us to store larger amounts of data (like your journal) directly on your device without sending it to a server.

## 2. Why we use them

We use these technologies to:

- Keep you signed in between sessions.
- Remember your visual preferences (like Dark Mode).
- Store your progress in courses and your private journal entries.

## 4. How to Manage Your Data

You can control or delete LocalStorage data through your browser settings:

- **Clear Cache/Data:** Deleting your browser history/cache will usually remove all MindFull data.
- **Incognito Mode:** Using a private window will prevent data from being saved after you close the tab.
- **Account Settings:** You can use the "Reset Account" or "Delete Data" options in your MindFull settings page.

## 5. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We encourage you to periodically review this page for the latest information on our privacy practices.
